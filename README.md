# Instagram-Music-Sheet
Instagram Music Sheet allows you to export your saved audio page into a spreadsheet for your preferred handling.

## Exporter 
- When running the main script (IMS exporter) for the first time, a browser window will appear.
Log into your instagram account and a .json file containing your login cookie will be produced,
allowing you to avoid having to log in again.

- Afterwards, the program will ask you scan your saved audios and give you the total amount.
From there, you can choose how many songs you wan't included on the spreadsheet.

## Updater

- The spreadsheet will be created as an .csv file and automatically update the metadata to
locate the song's genre and update the spreadsheet.
